# GeoPen User Research - Interview Template

## Purpose
Gather feedback from field geologists and core logging technicians about their current measurement workflows and requirements for GeoPen.

## Interview Structure

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Introduce project and purpose
- Explain interview format
- Get consent for recording (if applicable)

### Background Questions (10 minutes)
1. What is your role and experience level?
2. How long have you been working in field geology?
3. What types of projects do you typically work on?
4. How often do you measure core alpha and beta angles?

### Current Workflow (15 minutes)
1. What tools do you currently use for measuring core angles?
2. Walk me through your typical measurement process
3. What are the biggest challenges with current tools?
4. How long does a typical measurement take?
5. How do you record and manage measurement data?
6. What happens to the data after measurement?

### Pain Points (10 minutes)
1. What frustrates you most about current measurement tools?
2. What errors or mistakes commonly occur?
3. How do measurement errors impact your work?
4. What would make your job easier?

### Requirements and Expectations (15 minutes)
1. What accuracy do you need for alpha and beta angles?
2. How important is speed vs. accuracy?
3. What form factor would work best for field use?
4. How important is digital data transfer?
5. What features would be most valuable?
6. What would prevent you from using a new tool?

### GeoPen Concept (10 minutes)
1. Show GeoPen concept (description/mockup)
2. What are your initial thoughts?
3. What concerns do you have?
4. What features are missing?
5. What would make you want to use this tool?
6. What price point would be acceptable?

### Closing (5 minutes)
1. Any additional comments or suggestions?
2. Would you be interested in beta testing?
3. Can we follow up with questions?
